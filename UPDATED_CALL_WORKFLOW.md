## **â“ FAQ:**

### **Q: Do I have to log every call?**
**A:** No! Tap "Not Now" if you don't want to log it.

### **Q: Can I log a call later?**
**A:** Yes! Go to Call Logs screen and tap the + button.

### **Q: What if I don't have phone service?**
**A:** The dialer will still open, but the call won't connect. You can still log it manually.

### **Q: Does this use my phone minutes?**
**A:** Yes, it uses your regular phone plan/minutes, just like any normal call.

### **Q: Can I call from WhatsApp or other apps?**
**A:** No, this uses your device's default phone dialer only.

### **Q: What if the number is wrong?**
**A:** You can edit it in your device's dialer before calling, but you'll need to update it in the student's contact info for next time.
